Some Bradley-Approved Non-Classical Music

Electronica by BT

BT is one of my favorite artists. He is a classically trained pianist who pioneered the trance genre. Several of these tracks come from his album Electronic Opus, which combined electronic instruments with those of an orchestra.

  • Skylarking
  • Flaming June
  • Good Morning, Kaia (Kaia is his daughter)
  • Satellite
  • The Antikythera Mechanism
  • The Emergency

Electro Swing

Electro swing appeals to me because I enjoy the juxtaposition of the old and new. Some of my favorite artists are Caravan Palace, Parov Stelar, and 11 Acorn Lane.

  • Claudette, by 11 Acorn Lane
  • Sugar and Cream, by 11 Acorn Lane
  • Jolie Coquine, by Caravan Palace
  • Doin' the Jive (Glenn Miller), Swing Bot

Muse
