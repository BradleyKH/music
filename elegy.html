Elegy

in memory of Chris Scherf

Among the things which Chris valued are beauty, craftmanship, and redemption.

In memory of Chris, I have tried to craft something beautiful that communicates redemption.

In particular, Chris was fond of music written by the Taizé community.
There is a pivotal section of this elegy which features a harmonic progression common in Taizé music.

May this music play a redemptive role in the lives of those who grieve.

-Bradley
